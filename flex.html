About it

Regular Show is an animated series that follows the lives of Mordecai, a blue jay, and Rigby, a raccoon, two slackers who work as groundskeepers at a park. Despite their laziness and constant antics, the duo often finds themselves in bizarre, surreal situations that spiral out of control. The show’s humor blends absurdity with heart, exploring friendship, growing up, and the everyday challenges of life. Alongside their coworkers—Skips, Pops, Muscle Man, and Benson—the characters navigate strange events and outlandish conflicts. *Regular Show* is beloved for its unique blend of comedy, adventure, and quirky, yet relatable, characters.
